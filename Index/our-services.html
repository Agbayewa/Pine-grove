The walk in shower design on this project incorporates the same detailed wall tile pattern as the floor to ceiling vanity tile which gives this space a continuous design flow. The frameless glass enclosure assists this design by eliminating visual obstruction. The tile layout and design was one of the most intricate aspects of this project. From rough framing to finish tile location, each measurement was pre-determined in order to allow full tile sizes to span between the floating bench, niche and top of knee-wall to prevent awkwardly dimensioned tile cuts. A floating bench was incorporated into this shower by fastening an extremely durable bracket system to the rough framing allowing this detail to float without any need for additional front support. A 3cm white zen polished custom cut quartz stone was fabricated and installed onto the brackets. Our client selected a dual shower head system with an adjustable sliding bar and matching controls. We incorporated a robe hook into the custom shower glass which was cored through the glass. We are extremely fortunate to have such creative clients and architects. Architect: @curl_simitis Photographer: @goodhue.creative
This fully custom floating double vanity was the perfect choice for this space. The millwork was handcrafted with walnut and a clear matte coat finish. We made sure to align wood grains in a vertical pattern for a uniform design. We incorporated InvisiLED motion sensing lighting on the vanity underside to exaggerate the floating design as well as provide hands free lighting for nighttime use. A 3cm white zen polished quartz countertop was selected which goes great with the 4"-12" white floor to ceiling wall tile. Accents of oceanside glass - blue clouds tile were incorporated in the upper section of the wall to give the design a more intricate detail. Our client selected an italian imported faucet with 3 hole basin mixer and aerator to go along with the undermounted sinks. Large 40" recessed Robern magnifying mirror cabinets were selected to provide our clients with hidden storage and a sleek mirror design. A pair of walnut Cerno Merus LED wall sconces for each vanity station tie all of the design elements together and provide a beautifully lit space. A great deal of time and coordination was put into this portion of the project and we are very pleased with the final product. What we subconsciously appreciate is the expertise, thought and professional experience required to bring that creative design to fruition. These floating shelves were hand built from single sheets of finish grade ply. A double inside miter cut leaving one single layer of ply allows the wood to fold at each 90 degree angle revealing a continuous wood grain. Each shelf is then carefully sanded stained and finished prior to install. The shelves then slide into place on stud mounted blocks within the recessed shelf location.
